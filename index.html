Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book? expand_more
Most couples book 12–18 months in advance, especially for popular fall and spring Saturdays in Indianapolis. However, it never hurts to check if your date is still open!
Do you travel outside of Indianapolis? expand_more
Absolutely! While we love our Indy venues like Bottleworks and Laurel Hall, we travel worldwide. We have special destination packages for weddings outside of Central Indiana.
How long until we see our photos? expand_more
You'll receive a curated set of "Sneak Peek" photos within 24 hours of your wedding. Your full, final gallery will be delivered via a private online link within 4–6 weeks.
Can we customize a package? expand_more
Yes! We understand every wedding is unique. If our standard packages don't quite fit your needs, we're happy to create a custom proposal just for you.
